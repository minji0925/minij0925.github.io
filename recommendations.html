Anti Recommendations

Pretty self-explanatory: the opposite of recommendations. Read/watch if you are genuinely inclined to kill time!

  • Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros
    The first book is okay, the second book gets repetitive. Both books are very addictive but very unproductive (i.e. fun but cliche, most things are predictable and not very new). Consider reading if you want to get out of a reading slump!
  • Crazy Rich Asians by Kevin Kwan
    Same as above, but rom-com instead of fantasy. Maybe I have an inherent dislike for romance, but the plot for this one was also too shallow.
  • Norwegian Wood by Haruki Murakami
    A bit too depressing. I have an unpublished full-length review for this one that I might want to put out on the Internet.
  • Titanic - slightly controversial but I think it's overhyped
